Criteria

Evaluation criterias

  • Reach / Impact: Refers to the extent to which the contributor’s work has spread throughout the community.
  • Importance: Evaluates the significance of a contributor’s work within the community.
  • Effort: Effort assesses the amount of time and energy a contributor has put into their contributions.
  • Originality: Look at how unique or innovative a contributor’s work is.
